Homage to Form

Form does not fade. It persists throughout time.

Long after a maker is gone, form remains — carrying forward decisions shaped by material, constraint, and intent. This is how knowledge moves across generations: not through instruction, but through what endures.

At Vintage Tech, reverse engineering the past begins with form.
